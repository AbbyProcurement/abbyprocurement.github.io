About Us

ABBY Procurement Services is a company located in Miami, FL aimed to fulfill the need for industrial equipment and replacement parts for companies in the located in the Caribbean and Latin America.

Our experienced team along with a wide network of top brands manufacturers and suppliers, strive to provide our customers with timely service, ensuring the delivery of materials and equipment at the lowest total cost. ABBY specializes in sourcing, and supply chain management, always researching sources of supply, product availability, specifications, and suitable substitutes.

From our warehouse located in Medley, FL, we have the ability to consolidate orders from different vendors before shipping final destination, as well as, filling full container loads of products. In that regard, we assure that proper export documentation and compliances are met for all shipments.

Services

Purchasing services and procurement of industrial and replacement part with the ability to consolidate orders from different vendors or fill full container loads of products for export purposes.
